### **FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS**

**Q: When will the club open?**  
A: We anticipate soft opening in 9-10 months (Q4 2026), with full operations shortly thereafter.

**Q: What if I change my mind?**  
A: Deposits are refundable until club opening with 90-day written notice. After opening, memberships can be sold or transferred (with board approval).

**Q: Can I bring guests?**  
A: Yes! Members may bring guests at any time. Guest fees apply ($25-$50 per guest depending on amenities used). Founding members receive 50% off guest fees for life.

**Q: Is there a dress code?**  
A: Smart casual. We want members to be comfortable while maintaining the club's sophisticated atmosphere.

**Q: What about border crossings?**  
A: Point Roberts is accessed via a 30-minute drive from Vancouver, including a brief border crossing. We recommend NEXUS passes for frequent visitors (expedited crossing). The club is a "destination"‚Äîplan to spend 3-4 hours or a full day when you visit.

**Q: Are children welcome?**  
A: Absolutely. We offer junior memberships for children of members and family-friendly programming.

**Q: What happens if the club doesn't reach capacity?**  
A: The club is financially viable at 200 members (conservative scenario). At 250, it will be exceptionally strong. Initiation fees fund initial development, and dues cover ongoing operations.

**Q: Can I host my wedding or corporate event at the club?**  
A: Yes! Members have priority booking for private events. The waterfront setting is perfect for weddings, corporate retreats, and celebrations.
